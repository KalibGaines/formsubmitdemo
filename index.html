About

@ThomasMadeLLC. We believe there is a process to creating anything worth having.

A certain mentality that one must have in order to bring their dreams into fruition. We take pride in helping others reclaim their own dominion. ThomasMadeLLC is a safe place to explore endless opportunities and for one to discover their limitless potential. We offer our services within the realms of Apparel & Fitness, Expanding our territory in many other areas of life soon to come! As we expand our community of tomorrows leaders, we welcome you to be a part of our journey. WELCOME TO THE THOMASMADE FAMILY!


ThomasMadeLLC. Was founded by Tehran Thomas in loving memory of his father. An Arizona native who grew up competing in the realm of sports. A former state champion High School Quarterback (2015). Tehran then would take his talents to (2018 Big Sky Champions) UC Davis, where he was a D1 Running Back from 2017-2021. A decorated athlete whose career came to early end. Throughout his career, he was assisted by great mentors who taught him more about himself than he could possibly imagine. The goal through ThomasMadeLLC, is to take these experiences and share the lessons learned with today’s future.
